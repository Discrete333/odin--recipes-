Meatball

Description

Meatballs are a classic dish made from a variety of ground meats mixed with ingredients like breadcrumbs, herbs, and spices, formed into balls and cooked in various ways. They are versatile, easy to make in large batches, and can be served as a main dish or appetizer, or added to sandwiches, soups, or stews. They are a convenient and delicious option for any meal.

Ingrediants

  • Ground meat (such as beef, chicken, or lamb)
  • Breadcrumbs (either homemade or store-bought)
  • An egg (to help bind the mixture together)
  • Finely chopped herbs (such as parsley or basil)
  • Grated cheese (such as parmesan or romano)
  • Minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven or heat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine ground meat, breadcrumbs, egg, herbs, cheese, garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix together well.
  3. Roll the mixture into balls and place on a baking sheet or in a skillet.
  4. Bake or cook the meatballs until they are cooked through and browned.
  5. Serve hot with your choice of sauce or over pasta, rice, or in a sandwich.
