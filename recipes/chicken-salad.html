Chicken Salad

Description

Chicken salad is a delicious and satisfying dish that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you're looking for a quick lunch option or a tasty addition to your next picnic or potluck, chicken salad is sure to please. Made with tender, juicy chicken and a variety of crunchy, flavorful ingredients, this dish is both flavorful and satisfying. The creamy, mayonnaise-based dressing adds a rich and indulgent touch, while the diced celery, onions, and pickles provide a refreshing crunch. Plus, chicken salad is incredibly versatile and can be served in a variety of ways, from atop a bed of crisp lettuce to inside a fluffy sandwich roll. So next time you're in the mood for a delicious and satisfying meal, give chicken salad a try!

Ingrediants

  • 2 cups cooked, diced chicken
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1/4 cup diced pickles
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the diced chicken, mayonnaise, celery, onion, and pickles.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Stir until the ingredients are well combined and the chicken is evenly coated in the dressing.
  4. Serve chilled, either on top of lettuce or as a sandwich filling.
