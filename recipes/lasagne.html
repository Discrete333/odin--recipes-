Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of pasta, meat or vegetables, and cheese, all baked together in a casserole dish. It is a hearty and satisfying meal that is perfect for gatherings or for feeding a crowd. The layers of pasta, sauce, and cheese create a creamy and flavorful dish, while the inclusion of protein-rich ingredients like meat or vegetables make it a well-rounded meal. Lasagne is also a great option for meal prep, as it can be made in advance and reheated for an easy weeknight dinner. Overall, lasagne is a delicious and satisfying dish that is sure to be a hit with the whole family.

Ingrediants

  • Lasagne noodles (either pre-made or homemade)
  • Meat or vegetables (such as ground beef, sausage, chicken, or a combination of vegetables like zucchini, eggplant, and bell peppers)
  • Tomato sauce or marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg (optional, helps to bind the ricotta filling)
  • Chopped herbs (such as basil or parsley)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Mozzarella cheese (for the top layer)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and cook the lasagne noodles.
  2. Brown the meat or cook the vegetables and add tomato sauce or marinara.
  3. Mix the ricotta, parmesan, egg, herbs, salt, and pepper.
  4. Assemble the lasagne by layering the meat or vegetables, noodles, and ricotta mixture in a baking dish, ending with meat or vegetables on top.
  5. Cover with mozzarella cheese and bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Let the lasagne cool before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
